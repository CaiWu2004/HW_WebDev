Ingredients

  • Salt
  • Water
  • 1/2 lb Macaroni
  • 2 Tbsp (28g) Unsalted Butter
  • 2 Tbsp (18g) All-Purposed Flour
  • 1 Cup (240ml) Whole Milk
  • 2 Cups (180g) Cheese
    • 1/2 : 1/2 of Raclette and Chedder

Instructions

  • Season your boiling water generously with salt(2 tbsp).
  • Add your pasta of choiuce and cook juntil al dente.
  • Drain the pasta and set it to the side.
  • Add butter to a medium saucepan or any other pan on medium heat.
  • Once the butter is melted, add an equal part flour and mix for about 45 seconds.
  • Slowly add your whole milk into the mixture and keep whisking on medium heat until it thickens.
  • Introduce your cheese to the saucepan anmd whisk until velvety smooth.
  • Finish off the sauce by adding a little bit of creme fraiche.
  • Finally toss in your cooked pasta and coat it with sauce.
